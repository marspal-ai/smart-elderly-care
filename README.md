# marspal-smart-elderly-care智慧养老

#### 系统介绍
火星先驱 智慧养老平台
为积极应对人口老龄化，推动养老服务数字化转型，我们推出全新一代智慧养老综合服务平台。平台以“科技赋能、普惠便捷、协同治理”为核心理念，构建覆盖“政府监管、机构运营、社区服务、居家养老、多元参与”的全链条智慧养老生态体系，全面连接民政部门、街道社区、养老机构、助餐服务点、供应商与广大老年人，打造“线上+线下”融合、“服务+监管”一体的智慧养老新范式。

平台采用“一平台、多终端”架构，包含六大核心板块：

 **民政端** ：实现对养老服务的全方位监管与科学决策。支持查看老人能力评估和机构等级评定、补贴发放审核、安全预警监测、数据统计分析等功能，助力政府部门精准施策、动态监管、提升治理效能。

 **社区端** ：赋能街道与社区服务中心，实现辖区老人信息管理、助餐点管理、上门巡访、老年人福利申报等日常运营数字化，打通养老服务“最后一公里”。

 **养老机构端** ：为养老院、护理院提供智能化运营管理工具，涵盖入住管理、健康监测、护理计划、医养协同、餐饮服务、安全预警等模块，提升服务质量与运营效率。

 **商家端** ：开放接入家政、维修、医疗、康复、文娱等各类为老服务供应商，实现服务上架、订单管理、服务履约等全流程管理，促进养老服务市场化、专业化发展。

 **助餐点端** ：专为社区老年食堂、助餐配送点设计，支持餐品管理、订餐统计等功能，保障“舌尖上的养老”安全便捷。

 **移动端（小程序）** ：面向老人及家属提供便捷入口，支持服务预约、政策资讯、助餐订餐、健康档案查询等一站式服务，让科技更有温度。

平台深度融合物联网、大数据、人工智能与云计算技术，实现老人健康数据自动采集、异常情况智能预警、服务需求精准匹配、政府监管实时可视，真正构建“数据驱动、多方协同、服务闭环、安全可控”的智慧养老新格局。智慧养老，智享晚年。本平台致力于让每一位老年人都能享受到更安心、更舒心、更有尊严的高品质生活服务，助力政府打造现代化、可持续的养老服务治理体系。

### English version introduction

### System Introduction
To actively respond to the aging population and promote the digital transformation of elderly care services, the Mars Pioneer Intelligent Elderly Care Platform has launched a new generation of intelligent elderly care comprehensive service platform. The platform takes "technology empowerment, universal convenience, and collaborative governance" as its core concept, and constructs a full chain intelligent elderly care ecosystem covering "government supervision, institutional operation, community services, home-based elderly care, and diversified participation". It comprehensively connects civil affairs departments, street communities, elderly care institutions, meal service points, suppliers, and the elderly, creating a new paradigm of intelligent elderly care that integrates "online+offline" and "service+supervision".

The platform adopts a "one platform, multiple terminals" architecture, which includes six core modules:

Civil Affairs: Achieve comprehensive supervision and scientific decision-making on elderly care services. Support functions such as viewing elderly ability assessments and institutional level evaluations, subsidy distribution audits, safety warning monitoring, data statistical analysis, etc., to assist government departments in implementing precise policies, dynamic supervision, and improving governance efficiency.

Community end: Empower streets and community service centers to digitize daily operations such as elderly information management, meal assistance management, home visits, and elderly welfare declaration, connecting the "last mile" of elderly care services.

Elderly care institutions: Provide intelligent operation and management tools for nursing homes and nursing homes, covering modules such as occupancy management, health monitoring, nursing plans, medical and elderly care collaboration, catering services, safety warnings, etc., to improve service quality and operational efficiency.

Merchant end: Open access to various elderly service providers such as housekeeping, maintenance, medical care, rehabilitation, entertainment, etc., to achieve full process management of service listing, order management, service fulfillment, etc., and promote the market-oriented and professional development of elderly care services.

Meal Assistance Terminal: Designed specifically for community elderly canteens and meal assistance delivery points, it supports meal management, order statistics, and other functions to ensure the safety and convenience of "elderly care on the tip of the tongue".

Mobile (Mini Program): Provides convenient access for the elderly and their families, supporting one-stop services such as service appointments, policy information, meal assistance and ordering, and health record inquiries, making technology more accessible.

The platform deeply integrates IoT, big data, artificial intelligence, and cloud computing technologies to achieve automatic collection of elderly health data, intelligent warning of abnormal situations, precise matching of service needs, and real-time visualization of government supervision, truly building a new pattern of intelligent elderly care that is "data-driven, multi-party collaborative, service closed-loop, and safe and controllable". Smart retirement, enjoy your old age with wisdom. This platform is committed to providing every elderly person with high-quality living services that are more reassuring, comfortable, and dignified, and helping the government build a modern and sustainable elderly care service governance system.
